# AI Job Trend Dashboard (Power BI)

A Power BI dashboard built to analyze trends in AI-related job roles, skills demand, experience requirements, and geographic distribution, enabling data-driven insights into the evolving AI job market.

---

## Project Overview
This dashboard explores how AI job demand varies across roles, skills, experience levels, and locations. It helps identify emerging trends, high-demand skill sets, and workforce patterns relevant for job seekers, recruiters, and analysts.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze demand trends for AI and data-driven roles  
- Identify in-demand technical and analytical skills  
- Understand experience and seniority requirements  
- Explore geographic distribution of AI jobs  
- Track changes in job market dynamics over time  

---

## Key Dashboards & Analysis

### 1. Job Market Overview
- Total AI job postings analyzed
- Distribution by:
  - Job role  
  - Industry  
  - Employment type  
- Job posting trends over time

**Question Answered:**  
Which AI roles are most in demand, and how is the market evolving?

---

### 2. Skills Demand Analysis
- Most frequently required skills
- Skill demand by:
  - Job role  
  - Experience level  
- Core vs advanced skill comparison

**Insight Focus:**  
Highlights skills that consistently appear across high-demand AI roles.

---

### 3. Experience & Seniority Analysis
- Jobs by experience level:
  - Entry  
  - Mid  
  - Senior  
- Relationship between experience and skill complexity
- Role-wise experience distribution

---

### 4. Location & Industry Insights
- AI job distribution by:
  - City / region  
  - Industry sector  
- Regional concentration of AI opportunities

**Question Answered:**  
Where are AI jobs concentrated, and which industries are driving demand?

---

## Measures & Calculations
- Total Job Postings  
- Skill Frequency Count  
- Role-wise Job Share  
- Experience-level Distribution  
- Location-wise Job Density  

All metrics are interactive and update dynamically with filters.

---

## Tools & Skills Used
- Power BI Desktop  
- DAX (measures & calculated columns)  
- Power Query for data transformation  
- Data modeling  

---

## Key Insights
- Strong demand for roles combining AI with data analytics  
- Core skills appear consistently across multiple job categories  
- Mid-level experience roles dominate job postings  
- Certain regions and industries act as AI talent hubs  

---

## Recommendations
- Focus skill development on high-frequency, cross-role requirements  
- Align learning paths with experience-level demand  
- Monitor geographic trends for job mobility decisions  
- Use skill overlap to transition between AI roles  

---

## Use Cases
- Job market and workforce analysis  
- Career planning and skill gap identification  
- Recruitment trend analysis  
- Analytics and consulting interviews
