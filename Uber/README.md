# Uber Trip Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

An end-to-end analytics dashboard built using Power BI to explore Uber trip data and uncover actionable insights on ride performance, demand patterns, revenue trends, vehicle usage, payment behavior, and location dynamics.

---

## Project Overview
This interactive dashboard enables operations, strategy, and data teams to understand ride-sharing activity — including trip volume, revenue, vehicle performance, time patterns, and spatial behavior — supporting data-driven decision-making. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

---

## Objectives
- Analyze ride demand and supply patterns
- Track revenue and trip performance
- Explore vehicle type and payment method correlations
- Identify peak hours, high-demand locations, and trends
- Surface actionable insights for operational improvements

---

## Key Dashboards & Analysis

### 1. Trip Overview
- Total trips completed and cancelled
- Trips by:
  - Date and time
  - Day of week
  - Trip type (day vs night)
- Trends over time

**Business Question Answered:**  
When and where is Uber demand highest, and how consistent is trip completion? :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

---

### 2. Revenue Analysis
- Total revenue
- Average revenue per trip
- Revenue by:
  - Vehicle type
  - Payment method
- Revenue trends over months

---

### 3. Vehicle & Rider Patterns
- Trips by vehicle category (e.g., UberX)
- Distance and duration distribution
- Most preferred vehicle types
- Rider behavior segmentation

---

### 4. Location Insights
- Top pickup locations
- Top drop-off locations
- City or zone-level demand patterns

**Business Question Answered:**  
Which geographic regions drive the most trips and revenue, and where should supply focus be increased? :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

---

## Measures & Calculations
- Total Trips
- Completed vs Cancelled Rates
- Average Trip Revenue
- Distance and Duration Metrics
- Revenue by Category

All metrics are dynamic and respond to slicers for deep exploratory analysis.

---

## Tools & Skills Used
- Power BI Desktop
- DAX (measures & calculated columns)
- Power Query for data transformation
- Data modeling and relationships

---

## Key Insights
- Peak ride demand occurs during specific hours and days
- Certain vehicle types dominate trip volume and revenue
- Payment behavior varies by time and rider segment
- Location clusters with high pickup/drop activity help optimize driver allocation

---

## Business Recommendations
- Incentivize drivers for peak-hour participation
- Optimize dynamic pricing during high-demand intervals
- Focus marketing and driver incentives in high-value zones
- Tailor service offerings based on vehicle type profitability

---

## Use Cases
- Operations and supply planning
- Revenue trend analysis
- Decision support for pricing and promotions
- Analytics portfolio and interview showcase
